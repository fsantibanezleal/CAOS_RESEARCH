**Status: FIRST-LEVEL results; the deeper 2D-analog search continues.**

   X constraints = 1; the map is the coeff map of (a+bt)(c+dt+et^2)). Confirmed.
2. **[MV]** The FAITHFUL naive 2D lowering (linear x linear; the two natural
   constraints resultant ad-bc=1 AND middle ad+bc=1) is DEGENERATE: it forces
   ad=1, bc=0, a singular coordinate hyperplane, NOT a smooth A^2. The two

3. **[MV]** Direct 2-sheeted planar covers (u, v^2 + g(u)) have Jacobian 2v (never

   simplest level, exactly the tension the 3D construction resolves with its
   third slot.

   pole (from the simple map in (x,u,r)) cancelling the x^3 of the coordinate
   change; the cubic 3-sheeted cover needs the third dimension. The mechanism
   appears INTRINSICALLY >= 3D, independent evidence it does not transplant to 2D
   (consistent with our published rigidity/uniqueness theorems).
