# EXP-018 - Verdict: CONFIRMED (2026-07-21). The cascade record is closed

## Resolved chains

1. **Poisson conjecture: the full conjecture is FALSE.** Adjamagbo and van den Essen, "On the
   equivalence of the Jacobian, Dixmier and Poisson Conjectures in any characteristic"
   (arXiv:math/0608009; Acta Mathematica Vietnamica 32 (2007) 209-218) prove the equivalence of
   the three conjectures (any characteristic), building on Tsuchimoto and Belov-Kanel &
   Kontsevich. Since the full Jacobian conjecture is false (EXP-001), the full Poisson
   conjecture is false; the minimal failing Poisson dimension is open (as with Dixmier, where
   rank 1 stays open). The 2026-07-20 dossier flag is lifted.
2. **Symmetric/gradient Jacobian conjecture: FALSE in some dimension.** The de Bondt-van den
   Essen symmetric reduction (2005: JC reduces to cubic homogeneous maps with symmetric
   Jacobian, i.e. gradient maps) together with the verified Zhao equivalence (EXP-016) yields:
   there exists a dimension where a gradient Keller map (equivalently a Hessian-nilpotent
   quartic potential) is non-invertible. The explicit witness is the queued JCB-024 target.
3. **Sweep result.** No further published named open conjecture PROVEN to imply JC was found
   beyond the recorded set (Mathieu, Gaussian moments, vanishing, Image, Dixmier, Poisson,
   symmetric form). Adjacent items checked and excluded deliberately: the Markus-Yamabe
   conjecture (already refuted independently in 1997, not a corollary of this event); the
   homogeneous dependence problem (already settled by de Bondt in relevant dimensions); the
   factorial conjecture (implication direction does not propagate falsity of JC to it).
