# EXP-003: reproduction of the Jensen-Leykin n = 5 tropical prevariety (gfan 0.7)

Declared: 2026-07-24, BEFORE any run. Route: R1 calibration (tropical lane opener) /
feeds R2 and the CCB-030/031 frontier experiments. Backlog: CCB-029.

## Question

Does our wrapped gfan 0.7 installation (WSL Ubuntu 24.04, source build, binary hashes
recorded) reproduce, exactly, the published Jensen-Leykin n = 5 tropical prevariety
computations (arXiv:2301.02305v2, read in full; direct-read dossier 2026-07-24)?

## Motivation

JL25 is the live generic-mass frontier; every experiment we plan on their call
("different valuations or different versions of the Albouy-Chenciner equations")
depends on first reproducing their published data points bit-for-bit. Their paper
prints the exact commands; the f-vectors are published; a reproduction either
validates our toolchain or exposes a version/environment discrepancy: either outcome
is calibration value.

## Setup

gfan 0.7 built from the author tarball (math.au.dk/~jensen/software/gfan/
gfan0.7.tar.gz) with GMP + cddlib in WSL2 Ubuntu 24.04 (32 threads available);
tarball and binary SHA-256 recorded in the artifacts. System generated by gfan's
built-in `_nbody -N5 --masses --alsosymmetric --cayleymenger2`; mass substitutions
per the paper's sed pipeline; prevariety via `_tropicalprevariety --usevaluation
-j16 --mint --minx` (and --bits64 for the fast pass, per the paper).

## Falsifiable predictions

- **P1 (system inventory).** The `_nbody` generator emits the expected system: 20
  asymmetric AC + 10 symmetric AC + 5 planar Cayley-Menger polynomials in the 10
  distance variables with mass parameters (35 polynomials total), matching our cclib
  builders' inventory (spot-checked: our cleared f/g forms divide or equal gfan's up
  to monomial units and ordering).
- **P2 (powers-of-3 valuations).** With m_i -> t^(3^(i-1)) (valuations 1, 3, 9, 27,
  81), the computed prevariety has f-vector (1506, 4744, 8586, 8787, 4652, 993), and
  all unbounded ray directions have non-positive coordinates (the globally pointed
  recession cone JL25 report).
- **P3 (squares valuations).** With m_i -> t^(i^2) (valuations 1, 4, 9, 16, 25), the
  f-vector is (3586, 12012, 18531, 15625, 7072, 1357). (Component/comet analysis is
  NOT part of this experiment; it is the follow-up rung.)

## Success / failure criteria

SUCCESS: P1, P2, P3 all match exactly. FAILURE: any mismatch (recorded; then
diagnose version/flags/environment before any frontier experiment builds on the
toolchain). Caps: 3600 s per prevariety run (paper: 5 wall-clock minutes on 16
threads; we have 32).

## Method / environment

Runner: run.sh (WSL invocations) + run.py (orchestration, artifact capture, f-vector
parsing, exact spot-checks against cclib). Deterministic; no randomness. Artifacts
tee'd to `artifacts/` (raw gfan outputs, hashes, parsed f-vectors).
