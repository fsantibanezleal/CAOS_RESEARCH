## 1. Exclusion / obstruction (the spine)

The field's own history IS an exclusion program: HM06 excludes torus directions via
BKK; HJ11 via the tropical prevariety; Chang-Chen industrialize AK12's zw-diagrams and
die 93 of 117; the frontier is literally a list of 24 un-excluded cases. Our spine
(CC-P0..P3) reproduces these certificates in our exact pipeline, then attacks residual
diagrams one by one. Analogy to the jacobian program: a zw-diagram is to Smale 6 what a
staircase/window stratum is to JC(2): a combinatorial failure scenario killed by an
exact certificate; our transport/elimination discipline ports directly.

## 2. Anatomy / construction

The known "failure objects" to dissect:

- **Roberts' continuum** (one negative mass, Physica D 127 (1999)): the ONLY known
  continuum of relative equilibria. Reverse-engineer it exactly: reproduce the family
  in sympy, compute the AC-Jacobian rank drop along the curve, identify WHICH minor
  degenerates and what mass positivity does to that minor. Target: a structural lemma
  of the shape "the continuum mechanism forces a mass sign change", a new proof-shape
  feeding the spine. NEW PATH: CCB-013.
- **The AK12 codimension-2 exceptional variety** (n = 5): anatomize which would-be
  diagrams live there, and why equal masses (inside the variety!) nevertheless came out
  finite (MZ19). The variety is the construction skeleton the two-sided lens (6) reads.
- **The asymmetric equal-mass CCs at n >= 8** (MZ19 Theorem 2): what breaks the
  reflection symmetry that holds for all n <= 7? Secondary, but a cheap anatomy target
  once our listing instrument exists (CCB-009).

## 3. Recognition / classification

Reframe "finite for every mass vector" as recognition of the INCIDENCE VARIETY: let
$V \subset \{(m, r)\}$ be the CC incidence variety and $\Pi: V \to \{m\}$ the mass
projection; finiteness = quasi-finiteness of $\Pi$ (fibers 0-dim) off an explicit
subvariety. Moeckel 2001 and Dias 2026 (Dziobek-Veronese: the Dziobek stratum
RECOGNIZED as a determinantal variety isomorphic to a Veronese image, dimension then
certified by linear algebra) are exactly this lens; arXiv:1508.06593 ("New equations
for central configurations and generic finiteness") extends it. NEW PATH: exact LOCAL
dimension certificates of the planar incidence variety for n = 5, 6 at exact rational
sample points (Jacobian rank over QQ: cheap, machine-checkable; gives generic-dimension
evidence and, on irreducible strata with literature-backed irreducibility, genuine
generic-finiteness statements). CCB-015.

## 4. Invariant (try FIRST, cheapest leverage)

- **Critical-value rigidity**: normalized CCs are critical points of $U|_{I=1}$; on any
  CONNECTED continuum both $U$ and $I$ are constant. So every putative continuum lies
  in a level set {U = c}: two cheap scalar constraints the tropical machinery does not
  use explicitly (HJ11 carry $e_{IU}$ for exactly this reason). Exploit: add level-set
  slicing to per-diagram exclusions.
- **Hessian nullity beyond symmetry**: a continuum forces Morse-Bott degeneracy; the
  detector is exact nullity of the Hessian of $U|_{I=1}$ at a CC minus the symmetry
  dimension. Build the exact Hessian instrument, calibrate on the square / tetrahedron
  / collinear CCs (all exact from EXP-001), then evaluate AT exceptional masses:
  Palmore's degeneracies say nullity does jump at bifurcation masses; the question per
  diagram is whether it jumps ENOUGH. NEW PATH: CCB-014.
- **The J invariant** $J = U I^{1/2}/M^{5/2}$ (MZ19): the cross-normalization matcher
  for every census we run (already adopted).

## 5. Symmetry / representation theory

$S_n \times O(2)$ acts throughout; equal-mass strata carry the full $S_n$. Uses:
(a) isotypic decomposition of the Hessian at symmetric CCs explains count bifurcations
(Palmore) and feeds lens 4; (b) symmetry-adapted normal forms shrink per-diagram
eliminations: many of the 24 Chang-Chen residual diagrams have automorphisms; quotient
the elimination by the diagram automorphism group before Groebner (the jacobian
program's GL2-orbit reduction, EXP-039, is the ported pattern). NEW PATH: CCB-018.
Open analogy: WHY are all equal-mass CCs reflection-symmetric exactly up to n = 7
(MZ19)? A symmetric-criticality proof-shape would be a theorem-grade target once the
listing instrument reproduces the phenomenon.

## 6. The two-sided reading

Every surviving zw-diagram is an exclusion failure read forwards, a CONSTRUCTION
skeleton read backwards. The honest program must genuinely attempt the construction:
pick exact masses ON a residual Chang-Chen mass relation, and run certified local
continuation of the associated CC cluster (does a curve of solutions persist?). If a
segment ever certifies, Smale 6 is FALSE and the record must be ready to say so; the
expected outcome (per MZ19's equal-mass experience inside AK12's variety) is collapse
to isolated points, which itself sharpens the diagram's exclusion. NEW PATH: CCB-017
(gated on CCB-004, the exact transcription of the 24 cases).

## 8. Dimension / parameter ladder

Two ladders locate the hardness: the n-ladder (3 trivial, 4 machine-closed, 5 modulo
codim-2, 6 = 24 cases, 7 fully open) and the configuration-dimension ladder (collinear
= Moulton, closed by convexity; Dziobek dim n-2 = determinantal, generically closed;
full planar = hardest). The structural reason planar is hard: the planar stratum sits
BELOW the Dziobek stratum where the determinantal recognition (lens 3) degenerates.
NEW PATH (scoping): compute the zw-diagram count growth for n = 7 with the Chang-Chen
combinatorics to size the next frontier honestly before anyone climbs it. CCB-020.

## 9. At-infinity / boundary

The tropical weights ARE the at-infinity lens: a surviving ray encodes a cluster
hierarchy (subsets collapsing at rates $\omega$). Physical reading: collapse limits are
governed by CCs of the SUB-clusters with renormalized masses (Xia's small-mass
constructions; Moeckel's cluster analyses). NEW PATH: a recursive exclusion principle:
"if every strict-subset CC problem arising in a diagram's cluster tree is finite and
nondegenerate at the induced masses, the diagram dies". If that principle (or a
version) can be proved, the 24 residual cases reduce to finitely many smaller-n
nondegeneracy checks our instruments can run. Literature check FIRST (Moeckel's notes
cluster sections; Xia 1991): this may exist in partial form; novelty pass mandatory.
CCB-016.
