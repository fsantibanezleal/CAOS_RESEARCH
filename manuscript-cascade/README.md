Counterexample: a Source-Verified Cascade". The corollary list (Mathieu, Dixmier, Poisson,
Gaussian moments, vanishing, Image, symmetric form), what remains open, and the verification
method (records EXP-001/016/018). v0.02 (2026-07-22) adds the EXPLICIT dimension-48 witness

Hessian-nilpotent quartic potential and an exact Q(i) collision, upgrading the
symmetric/gradient and vanishing rows from existential to explicit. Credit for every
corollary belongs to the original counterexample (and, for the witness input, to Thompson's
dimension-24 cubic-homogeneous form, verified in-house with its nilpotency index corrected
17 -> 18); this document contributes the source-verified status map and the construction.
